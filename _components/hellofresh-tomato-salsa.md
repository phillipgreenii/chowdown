utensils:
- 1 medium bowl

ingredients:
- 1 Roma Tomato, diced
- 1 tablespoon yellow onion, chopped
- 1/4 ounce Cilantro, roughly chopped
- juice from 1/2 lime
- 1 pinch of salt
- 1 pinch of pepper

directions:
- In a medium bowl, combine tomato, cilantro, onion, lime juice, and a pinch of salt and pepper.
